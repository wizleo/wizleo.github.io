WHO WE ARE

We are a team of 3 students from ALX Africa passionate about solving Africas problems with technology
For this project we tackled an issue that affects many parts of africa, that is poor sanitation
We noticed that in most cases you find that people just dont know where to get the right cleaning services easily
so we made it easier for people all over africa to get in touch with cleaning services providers in their areas with a simple click of a button.
In addition to this, we offer service providers a platform to easily market and provide thier cleaning services.
